Welcome to Chollerton Tearooms

Tearooms

Our tearooms are the home of british traditions, an elegant place for you and your loved ones to discover the british culture through one of its most representative things: tea. Not only that you can sit down and sip from a cup of natural tea prepared by our experienced staff, but you can also enjoy a healthy breakfast by choosing from our wide range of products: from pastry to salads, we provide you anything you need to start your day.

Craft Shop

All of your essential arts and crafts supplies are available to buy at our craft shop. For all types of creative minds and aspiring artists. No matter how unusual your hobby is, in our craft shop you can find a wide range of supplies: from basic tools to rare objects. Moreover, if you're looking for various decorations, here is the right/perfect place to find something suitable for you and your house.

Village Store

Find all the supplies you need to cook a healty meal or just try out the vast selection of stone cooked steaks. Come in for the best grocery experience, because here you will find fresh and ecological products, right from the nearby farms. If you don't want to prepare a meal at home, our cooked dishes are excelent for those who want to go back in time and experience the real taste of food.

Post Office

Deliver and recieve mail from the confort of our in-house post office. We ship all over the UK and international, provided you let us know beforehand. Besides the clasic post office, we can offer you the express service, in case you have to send something faster than usual. Your mail will arrive at the specified destination within 24 hours, anywhere in the world, all for just a small additional fee.

Bed and Breakfast

Relax in one of our cozy rooms for the night and enjoy a delightful breakfast in the morning. Our products are suited to vegans and vegetarians and hand-picked to ensure the best experience for our guests. We believe Chollerton Tearooms is the right chioce for visitors who are searching for a quiet and peaceful place to spend the night. Facilities: 24/7 reception, wi-fi, fax, ironing service, phone in each room, bar, private bathroom and room service.
